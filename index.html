Be healthy

With more than 27 vitamins and minerals, 13 amino acids, and more antioxidants than even green tea, yerba mate is among the most nutrient-rich plants on the planet. It is metabolism-boosting, anti-inflammatory and immunotherapeutic. The Guarani people of Paraguay, who first used yerba mate thousands of years ago, called it the Drink of the Gods and it formed the basis of their medicine.
