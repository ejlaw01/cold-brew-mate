Our Mates

Why drink mate?

Feel good

The effect of mate (MAH-tay) is at once envigorating and calming. Imagine the energy of coffee, the relaxation of tea, and the euphoria of chocolate all in one. It's easy on the stomach and curbs hunger while increasing mental clarity. Best yet, there's no crash and caffeine-sensitive people experience less negative side effects than with other sources of caffeine. You really have to try it to believe it.

Be healthy

With more than 27 vitamins and minerals, 13 amino acids, and more antioxidants than even green tea, yerba mate is among the most nutrient-rich plants on the planet. It is metabolism-boosting, anti-inflammatory and immuno-therapeutic. The Guarani people of Paraguay, who first used yerba mate thousands of years ago, called it the Drink of the Gods and used it as the basis of their medicine.

Live sustainably

Our organic yerba mate is all shade-grown. Not only is shade-grown mate the most flavorful and nutrient-dense, but its agriculture helps redevelop deforested areas. We source from Fair Trade farmers in Brazil and Paraguay. These farms double as nurseries for native hardwood rainforest trees while providing a stable income and healthy working conditions. Our packaging is post-consumer and biodegradable.
